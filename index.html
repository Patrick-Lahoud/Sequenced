How to Play

You receive a random number each round.

Your goal: Place it into an ordered sequence.

Click on a gap to insert the number.

If the sequence stays in order, you continue!

One mistake = Game Over.

Survive all rounds to win!

Tip: Think ahead! Choose the best placement before locking in.
